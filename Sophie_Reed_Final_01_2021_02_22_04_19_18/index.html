STAR GENERATOR

DESCRIPTION:

This is a GUI, or a generative web application I created in p5js. Certain variables of the star I created can be manipulated by the interface on the right side of the screen.

PARAMETERS:

These are the custom controls I added to customize my star function. You can change the sliders to change the GUI!

  • points: this adjusts how many points the star has.
  • stroke: this changes how thick the black border is on the star.
  • radius1: this manipulates the radius.
  • radius2: this manipulates the radius as well.
  • rotate: this function rotates the star.
  • r: this manipulates the red tones in the middle of the star.
  • g: this manipulates the green tones in the middle of the star.
  • b: this manipulates the blue tones in the middle of the star.
  • alpha: this changes how pigmented the color in the middle of the star is.
  • color: this manipulates the background color.
